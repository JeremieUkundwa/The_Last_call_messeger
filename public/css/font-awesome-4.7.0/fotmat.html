We wish you blessings and God's gracious peace.

"We thank the Lord with all the heart that we have precious light to present before the people, and we rejoice that we have a message for this time which is present truth. The tidings that Christ is our righteousness has brought relief to many, many souls, and God says to His people, “Go forward.” - 1SM 357.1

The Three Angels Messages, is the message we proclaim. We invite those who are ready to read it. May God bless you. The message is grouped into three main categories:

  1. Justification by grace through faith that invites us to repent and receive Jesus Christ our righteousness who becomes our life.

  2. The prophecy that reveals the current times, we observe the signs that Jesus foretold and its fulfilment.

  3. Healthful messages (temperance) that teach us to take care of our bodies as temples of the Holy Spirit. We urge everyone to take heed to this message. We pray for God's continuous presence. May the leading of the Holy Spirit be with every reader. Maranatha!
